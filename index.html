HOW TO PLAY!

1. Roll the dice to start your turn. If you roll a 1, your turn ends and you lose all your points for that turn. If you roll anything other than a 1, the number on the dice will be added to your current score.
2. Decide whether to roll again or hold. If you hold, your turn ends and your current score is added to your total score.
3. The first player to reach the target score wins!
Remember: You can always check the rules again by clicking the "i" button.
Good luck and have fun!
